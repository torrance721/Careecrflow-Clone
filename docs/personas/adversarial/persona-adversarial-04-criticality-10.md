## 预期回答示例

**问题**: "What is the single most important governance mechanism you would advocate for to ensure the board maintains strategic oversight without micromanaging the executive team?"

**回答**: "The most critical governance mechanism I would advocate for is a structured Strategic Review Cadence with clear escalation thresholds. Specifically:

1. **Quarterly Deep Dives**: Each quarter, one board meeting should be dedicated entirely to a single strategic theme (e.g., AI transformation, international expansion, M&A pipeline) with pre-read materials distributed 2 weeks in advance.

2. **Real-time Dashboard Access**: Board members should have read-only access to a curated executive dashboard showing 5-7 leading indicators (not lagging KPIs) that signal strategic health. This enables informed questions without operational interference.

3. **Escalation Framework**: Define clear thresholds (e.g., >15% deviation from plan, regulatory risk above X, key executive departure) that trigger mandatory board notification within 48 hours.

4. **Executive Session Protocol**: Every board meeting should end with 30 minutes of executive session (without management) to discuss concerns candidly.

I implemented this framework at my previous company, and it reduced board-management friction by 60% while improving strategic decision quality, as measured by our post-mortem analysis of major decisions."
